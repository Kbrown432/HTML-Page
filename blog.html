Just Got Back from Portugal!

POSTED: June 24, 2013

We visited beautiful Portugal in June of 2013 and had a wonderful trip.

Lisbon is a gorgeous city, full of plazas, cafes, museums, and nightlife. Not to mention the restaurants! Unfortunately, we didn't arrive until June 12, thus missing the Portugal Day holiday celebration, which is every June Tenth.

Our favorite part of the trip was enjoying the views of the city from Tram 28. We had heard from other travelers that the best time to ride the tram is in the early morning, from 6:ooam to 9:00am, before the crowds are heavy. This turned out to be great advice.

On our final day, we wanted to visit the beaches in Cascais, about 20 miles wes of Lisbon, but our departing flight was at 3:00pm, so there just wasn't enough time. All the excuse we need to return for another visit in the future!

Tired but happy, we arrived back in Chicago at 7:00am on June 19.
